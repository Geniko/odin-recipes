Three-ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies

Do you have peanut butter? Do you have at least 2 eggs? Do you have sugar? Then you can have peanut butter cookies in your near future, my friend.

Ingredients:

  • 18 ounces of peanut butter; there are 18oz jars sold
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and 1/4 cups sugar
  • An additional 1/2 cup sugar for rolling the cookies in

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350F.
  2. Add 1 and 1/4 cup sugar, the peanut butter, and eggs together in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Once incorporated, roll dough-like mixture into inch-sized balls.
  4. Add the sugar set aside for rolling in a bowl and coat your peanut butter balls.
  5. Try and place the balls on a cookie sheet about the same width apart as you made the cookies.
  6. Cooking time is around 12 minutes or until the cookies are slighly more brown and cracking on top.
  7. These cookies are pretty delicate coming out of the oven, so wait a couple minutes after taking them out; then, carefully lay them on a cooling rack.
  8. Wait . . . yes, wait - for about 10 minutes.
  9. Now enjoy.

Makes around 35 cookies.
