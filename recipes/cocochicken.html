Coconut Chicken Tenders

I find that coconut flakes make a great alternative to breadcrumbs for baking chicken tenders. My preference is to use thigh meat (or dark meat) cut into tenders, but if you want use breast meat, go right ahead. Breast meat just has a habit of drying out faster.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb of chicken thighs cut into tender size - chicken should not be frozen
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups (at least) of unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 2 tsp of salt
  • (optional) Try black pepper, garlic, and paprika to spice up the coating.*

*Honestly, use whatever spices you deem would be delicious.

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Put some aluminum foil onto a baking sheet and lightly grease with oil.
  3. Take two bowls: in one, crack the two eggs and whisk them; in the other, put your coconut flakes and any spices.
  4. First, dip the tenders in the egg bowl, then the roll them around in the coconut flakes.
  5. Transfer to lined baking sheet and cook for 20 minutes.
